Our 1995 Nautique

Thanks to all of the donations and fundraising, we were able to purchase a incredibly beautiful 1995 Nautique. The boat has low hours, perfect pass and a GT-40 engine. This boat will be perfect for our team over the next few years as we continue to grow and develop as skiers and as a club.

Since our team is primarily a self-funded team, we are incredibly thankful for all donations recieved. If you would like to donate you can do so by mailing us a check, using Venmo (@MSU-waterski), or PayPal below. Below we also have a list of items our team could use if you have any spares lying around. Everything is appreciated. As always, feel free to reach out if you have questions!

We do prefer checks (especially for larger donations) since PayPal will charge us 2.9% + $0.30 per donation. Write out checks to Michigan State Waterski Club, mail to TJ Lynch (address will be posted soon). And shoot us a quick email at msuwaterski@gmail.com so we can keep an eye out for it.

Our team could use these items new or used if you have extra lying around:
  • Trick skis
  • Jump helmets
  • Jump suits
