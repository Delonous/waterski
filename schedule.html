2017 Schedule

Head to Head

The Annual UMSU Head to Head is a great way to start off the season with a friendly competition between friendly rivals. U of M and MSU warm up for the season ahead. This is our most relaxed tournament with appearances from other teams such as GVSU, EMU, and WMU.

When: Labor Day - Monday, September 4th

Location: Tivoli Gardens, 10393 Jewell Road, Gaines, MI 48436

Ripfest

Michigan State Spartans host their own annual tournament, Ripfest! This is a great tournament to attend with representation from many midwest teams competing to qualify for conferences. This tournament skiiers are able to show their true potential as they get accustomed to the Collegiate Tournament style of competition.

When: Friday, September 8th - Sunday, September 10th

Location: Tivoli Gardens, 10393 Jewell Road, Gaines, MI 48436

Great Lakes Conferences

The Great Lakes Conference Championship Tournament includes some of the top ranked teams in the Conference competing for one of the eight spots in the Midwest Regional Tournament. Schools have to have a total of team score that qualifies for this tournament.

When: Friday, September 22nd - Sunday, September 24th

Location: TBA

Regionals

The Midwest Collegiate Regionals is the largest tournament in the regular fall season. The top eight teams from the Great Lakes Conference and the top eight from the Great Planes Conference compete to determine who will be the best in the Midwest and who can qualify for Nationals. A longer road trip is required for this tournament, carpools leave on Thursday night, with skiing all weekend.

When: Thrusday, September 28th - Sunday, October 1st

Location: PITS Lake 5202 W Harristown Blvd, Decatur, IL 62522

Collegiate Water Ski Nationals

Collegiate Waterski Nationals are technically outside of the regular fall season, but it is the result of all the hard work and talent that is put into all the tournaments previous. Division 1 Nationals is restricted to only the top couple of teams from each region and then Division 2 consists of the 3-5th teams from each region. Every year a few wild card teams are chosen to attend as well. This is a extended weekend trip with lodging in hotels and transportation up to team's preference.

When: Wednesday, October 11th - Sunday, October 15th

Location: Bennett's Boat and Ski, 18605 Barnett Rd, Zachary, LA 70791
